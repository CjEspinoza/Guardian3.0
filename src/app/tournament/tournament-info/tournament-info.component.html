REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN

The following are the requirements for players and teams to submit to be allowed to enter our tournament:

  • Teams must consist of 6 players. 5 active players and one substition player.
  • Players age must range from 15 and above to join.
  • Teams must specify which games you would like to join.
  • Submit the list of the player's in game name, full name, team logo and team name in our official Guardian Tournament facebook page.

TOURNAMENT MECHANICS

  • For all the esports games in the tournament, there are no limits on how many teams can join the game's tournament.
  • Out of all the teams that entered their esports games, They will be divided into to groups to face off, only the best teams in both groups can advance.
  • Out of those teams, only the top 8 will proceed to our elimination stage.
  • The team seeding will be determined based on team standings or team wins and loses.
  • In the elimination stage, teams will play one game to determine who will advance to the next round.
  • In the semifinal stage, the teams that would advance would once again play one game to determine who will reach the finals.
  • The last 2 teams will face off in a best of 3 series. The first team to win 2 games will be crowned as the Guardian Tournament Champions.
